Zambian Traditional Dishes

Chikanda

Recipe: Ground orchid tubers, peanuts, salt, chili.

Preparation: Mix ground tubers and groundnuts, cook over fire while stirring until firm like cake. Let it set and slice.

Okra Soup

Recipe: Fresh okra, tomatoes, onions, salt, cooking oil.

Preparation: Slice okra and boil with tomatoes and onions until slimy. Season and serve with nshima.

Kalembula (Sweet Potato Leaves)

Recipe: Fresh sweet potato leaves, onions, tomatoes, oil, salt.

Preparation: Boil leaves slightly, drain water, fry onions and tomatoes, add leaves, stir and simmer.

Nshima

Recipe: Mealie meal (maize flour), water.

Preparation: Boil water, stir in mealie meal to make porridge, add more flour and stir until thickened.

Kablagenti Beans

Recipe: Dried beans, tomatoes, onions, salt, oil.

Preparation: Boil beans until soft. Fry tomatoes and onions, add beans, season, and simmer.

Sweet Potato in Groundnut Soup

Recipe: Sweet potatoes, groundnut powder, water, salt.

Preparation: Boil potatoes, add peanut powder, stir well and cook until thick.

Bondwe

Recipe: Amaranth leaves, tomatoes, onion, oil, salt.

Preparation: Cook chopped leaves with onion and tomato until tender.

Wild Mushroom

Recipe: Wild mushrooms, groundnuts, tomato, salt.

Preparation: Cook mushrooms in water, add groundnut powder and boil until done.

Kapenta

Recipe: Dried kapenta, tomatoes, onions, oil.

Preparation: Soak kapenta, fry with tomato and onion until cooked.

Vinkubala (Caterpillars)

Recipe: Dried caterpillars, onions, tomato, oil, salt.

Preparation: Soak and wash, fry with onion and tomato until tender.

Katapa (Cassava Leaves)

Recipe: Cassava leaves, groundnuts, tomatoes, salt.

Preparation: Pound leaves, cook with groundnuts and tomato until soft.

Ifisashi

Recipe: Greens, groundnuts, salt.

Preparation: Cook greens in water, add peanut powder and simmer until thick.

Samp

Recipe: Samp (crushed maize), water, salt.

Preparation: Boil until soft. Can be served with beans or soup.

Dry Fish

Recipe: Dried fish, tomato, onion, oil.

Preparation: Soak fish, fry with tomato and onion.

Village Chicken

Recipe: Village chicken, tomato, onion, salt, oil.

Preparation: Boil chicken until tender, fry with onion and tomato.

Busala

Recipe: Pumpkin leaves, groundnuts.

Preparation: Cook leaves with groundnuts until soft.

Impwa (Eggplant)

Recipe: Garden eggplants, onion, tomato, oil.

Preparation: Boil or fry with tomato and onion.

Snacks

Vitumbuwa (Fritters)

Recipe: Flour, sugar, yeast, luke-warm water.

Preparation: Mix ingredients, let rise, deep fry until golden brown.

Scones

Recipe: Flour, butter, sugar, baking powder, milk.

Preparation: Mix dough, cut into rounds, bake until golden.

Roasted Maize

Preparation: Roast fresh maize over open fire until brown.

Cassava Chips

Recipe: Cassava, salt, oil.

Preparation: Slice thinly and deep fry until crisp.

Sweet Potato Chips

Recipe: Sweet potatoes, oil, salt.

Preparation: Peel, slice thinly, and fry.

Michopo (Street Meat Kebab)

Recipe: Meat chunks, salt, pepper, oil.

Preparation: Grill on skewers, serve with onion salad.

Roasted or Boiled Groundnuts

Preparation: Roast dry or boil fresh with salt.

Drinks

Chibwantu

Recipe: Fermented maize or millet, water.

Preparation: Soak and ferment, strain, and serve chilled.

Tobwa

Recipe: Maize, sugar, water, ginger.

Preparation: Cook maize with sugar and ginger, cool and ferment lightly.

Mabuyu Drink

Recipe: Baobab fruit, sugar, water.

Preparation: Blend baobab pulp, strain, sweeten.

Tamarind Drink

Recipe: Tamarind pods, sugar, water.

Preparation: Soak pods in hot water, strain, and add sugar.

Mukonyo

Recipe: Finger millet, water.

Preparation: Prepare like porridge, cool and ferment. Refreshing and healthy.
